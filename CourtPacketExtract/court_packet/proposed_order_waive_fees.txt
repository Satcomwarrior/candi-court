THIS MATTER came before the Court on the Motion and Declaration for Waiver of
Filing Fees filed by Petitioner William Orley Miller Jr.  The Court considered the
declaration and financial statement submitted by Petitioner.  Being fully advised, the Court
makes the following findings and order:

FINDINGS

1. Petitioner has demonstrated financial hardship and inability to pay the filing fee
without substantial hardship.

2. Pursuant to General Rule 34, indigent persons may be excused from payment of
filing fees and surcharges when they are unable to pay.

ORDER

IT IS ORDERED that Petitioner’s Motion to Waive Filing Fees is GRANTED.  The Clerk
of Court shall accept all filings in this matter without payment of the filing fee or surcharges.
